# Meditation Retreat Nepal

Nepal is one of the world’s most supportive environments for reflection: quieter rhythms, Himalayan nature, and traditions that value mindfulness.

## Who this is best for
- People feeling mentally overloaded or “stuck”
- Beginners who need clear guidance
- Experienced practitioners who want depth and nature immersion

## How to choose the right retreat
- Small groups
- Clear daily schedule
- Integration support (journaling + guidance)
- Transparent pricing and booking
