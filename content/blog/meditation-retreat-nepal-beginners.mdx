# Meditation Retreat Nepal for Beginners

If you're new to meditation, a retreat can feel intimidating. Nepal is one of the best places to begin because the environment naturally supports calm and reflection.

## What beginners need most
- Simple guidance and a predictable schedule  
- Safety and emotional support  
- Gentle progression (not intensity)

## How to choose the right retreat
Look for small groups, clear daily flow, and time for integration.
