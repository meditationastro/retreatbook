# How to Find Your Life Purpose (Without Pressure)

Purpose is usually discovered through patterns, not sudden revelations.

## A simple framework
1. Values: what matters even when it's hard  
2. Energy: what leaves you feeling alive  
3. Service: what helps others

## Next step
Use a 7-day experiment approach and record what changes.
